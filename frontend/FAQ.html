Frequently Asked Questions

For Clients

How does the Smart Matching System work?

The system connects clients with trusted manicure and pedicure specialists based on location, availability, and preferences. Clients can search for specialists, view their profiles, and book appointments directly through the platform.

How do I sign up as a client?

To sign up, click on the "Sign Up" button, select "Client" as your role, and fill out the required details (e.g., name, email, location). Once registered, you can start browsing specialists and booking appointments.

For Specialists

How do I sign up as a specialist?

Click on the "Sign Up" button, select "Specialist" as your role, and fill out the required details (e.g., name, email, location, certifications). Once approved, you can start accepting appointments.

What information do I need to provide during registration?

You'll need to provide personal details, proof of certification, a profile photo, and a description of your services. You may also need to set your availability and pricing.

General Questions

What services are available on the platform?

The platform offers a variety of manicure and pedicure services, including basic, gel, acrylic, nail art, and more. Specialists can also offer additional services like hand and foot massages.

How do I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support through the "Contact Us" page or by emailing support@smartmatchingsystem.com. We're available 24/7 to assist you.
